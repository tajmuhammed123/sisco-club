About Sisco Arts and Sports Club

Founded with a vision to inspire talent and nurture community spirit, Sisco Arts and Sports Club is a hub where passion for sports meets love for the arts.

  • A vibrant platform that promotes both athletic excellence and cultural expression.
  • Home to thrilling tournaments like VPL and artistic showcases that bring people together.
  • Dedicated to encouraging youth participation, teamwork, and creativity across football, dance, music, drama, and more.

At Sisco Arts and Sports Club, we believe in building a stronger, united community through the spirit of competition, creativity, and collaboration. Every event we organize is a step toward empowering individuals and celebrating talent in all its forms.

🏆 VPL Season 7 Champions – Team Seven Star

The 2025 edition of the VPL Season 7, hosted by Sisco Arts and Sports Club, was a thrilling football tournament filled with passion, energy, and unforgettable moments.

  • Team Seven Star showcased incredible skill and determination to secure the championship title.
  • From the group stages to the finals, their consistency and teamwork stood out as the hallmark of true champions.
  • The victory was celebrated not just as a win, but as a symbol of unity, hard work, and the spirit of football in 2025.

VPL Season 7 will be remembered as a milestone event, bringing together athletes, fans, and the community to honor the beautiful game. The triumph of Seven Star symbolizes hard work, dedication, and the true spirit of sportsmanship.
